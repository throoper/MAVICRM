What is this demo?
An in house CRM to align on features, page views, and kinds of functionality before beginning to actually build. 

Who is this demo for?
Company Leadership and CS lead to align on what functionality we want before involving our engineering team

Core Use Cases
- View And Edit individual information for client organizations (Notes, Contracts, Talent, Issues)
- View and Edit Individual issues that occur (Resolution Steps, Associated Client / Talent)
- View Master List of Clients
- View Master list of Issues
- Reports (reports on clients and issues that can be manually generated or auto generate on specific intervals/triggers) 
- Ability to auto generate communications with clients via email on certain time intervals

Key Entities
- Contracts
- Companies
- Talent
- Internal Team Members
- Issues/Tickets

Core Workflows
- Update a company (add a contract, edit and add to notes)
- Create, Update, and Resolve an issue/ticket
- Check in with client (send email to check in on specified time intervals)
- View, and export key reports

Existing Tools
- Google Sheets

In Scope
- Demo versions of features to align on page views and workflows
- Populated with demo data

Out of Scope
- Actual functionality, integrations, authentication, etc
- Actual working database, real data, etc

Core Users
- Customer Success Managers for functionality (updating company information, managing issues/tickets, producing reports)
- Leadership (Viewing critical issues and report artifacts)
